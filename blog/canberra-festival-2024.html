Canberra Wine Festival 2024: A Complete Guide

March 15, 2024 Canberra Events

The Canberra Wine Festival 2024 is set to be the most exciting wine event of the year in Australia's capital. This comprehensive guide will help you make the most of this spectacular celebration of Canberra's thriving wine scene.

Event Overview

Held from April 12-14, 2024, at the picturesque Commonwealth Park, the Canberra Wine Festival brings together over 50 local wineries, showcasing the best of the region's cool climate wines. The festival is a celebration of Canberra's unique terroir and the passionate winemakers who call this region home.

Featured Wineries

This year's festival will feature an impressive lineup of wineries, including:

  • Clonakilla - Renowned for their Shiraz Viognier
  • Lark Hill - Specializing in biodynamic wines
  • Shaw Vineyard Estate - Known for their premium cool climate varieties
  • Mount Majura Vineyard - Award-winning Riesling producers
  • Helm Wines - Masters of Canberra's signature varieties

Special Events

Masterclasses

Don't miss these exclusive masterclasses:

  • Cool Climate Wine Tasting with Tim Kirk (Clonakilla)
  • Food and Wine Pairing Workshop with local chefs
  • Riesling Masterclass with Ken Helm

Live Entertainment

The festival features live music throughout the weekend, with performances from local artists and bands. The evening sessions are particularly popular, offering a magical atmosphere under the stars.

Food Pairings

Local restaurants and food vendors will be offering specially curated menus to complement the wines. Highlights include:

  • Artisan cheese boards from local producers
  • Gourmet food trucks serving regional specialties
  • Pop-up restaurants by Canberra's top chefs

Tips for Visitors

  1. Purchase tickets in advance to avoid queues
  2. Arrive early for the best selection of wines
  3. Bring a water bottle to stay hydrated
  4. Consider booking accommodation early as hotels fill up quickly
  5. Download the festival app for real-time updates and maps

Getting There

The festival is easily accessible by public transport, with regular buses running from the city center. Parking is limited, so we recommend using public transport or ride-sharing services.

Ticket Information

Tickets are available in several tiers:

  • General Admission: $45 (includes 10 tasting tokens)
  • VIP Experience: $120 (includes premium tastings and masterclass access)
  • Weekend Pass: $80 (access to all three days)
